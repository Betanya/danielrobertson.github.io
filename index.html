Hello and welcome! I recently graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and currently work at IBM Austin as a software engineer, building the back-end web services which power the Support Portal Next Generation, which launched a beta in March. My daily routine involves creating and working with REST APIs using Java, SQL, and lots of software testing including unit and integration tests. I also enjoy using and learning Node.js and Python. Follow me on GitHub!

When I'm not at my computer, I enjoy the outdoors and drumming. The weather is beautiful year-round, enabling outdoor activities like kayaking on Lake Austin or bicycling. Austin has a thriving startup and tech entrepreneur culture too, where I enjoy learning, networking and meeting new people. As a musician, The Live Music Capital of the World offers endless opportunities for me whether its seeing a live tour show downtown or the local symphony!
